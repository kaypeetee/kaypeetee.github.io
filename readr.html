All of your books and friends, In one place

Readr is a social media app designed for bookworms and avid readers. With a sleek and intuitive interface, Readr provides an immersive reading experience with features such as personalized recommendations, bookmarking, and messaging. Whether you're looking for your next favorite novel or want to engage in lively discussions about literature, Readr has something for every book lover.

Connect, Share, Dive in

Share your reading progress and opinions on books you're currently reading. Discover new books and articles through personalized recommendations based on your reading history and interests. Connect with other readers who share similar interests and engage in discussions about your favorite books and authors. Create and join reading groups where you can discuss books with other members and participate in group readings.
